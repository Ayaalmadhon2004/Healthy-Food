### 2. Browse Recipes

The **Browse Recipes** page is your hub for discovering meals that fit your lifestyle. Filter by Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, or Snacks, view prep time, calories, and add favorites to your cart. Each recipe in the cart keeps track of its **name, quantity, and calorie value**.

Click on any recipe to view full details: ingredients, instructions, servings, and nutritional info per serving. You can **Log this Meal** to track calories or **Add to Cart** to save it for later.

### 6. Gaza Kitchen Community

The **Gaza Kitchen Community** helps families locate free meals across Gaza. Filter by area, check distribution times, and see the meals served today. Each kitchen listing includes capacity, access info, and contact details.

The app also provides a **Family Nutrition Guide**, offering practical tips:

* Add vegetables to meals to increase volume and nutrition.
* Save bread for breakfast and reuse broth to retain nutrients.
* Prioritize children, pregnant, and nursing mothers for essential nutrition.
* Visit multiple kitchens for variety and balance.
